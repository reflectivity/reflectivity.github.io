Video record available per contacting the working group for permission

Soleil / SIRIUS beamline - Soft Interfaces and Resonant Investigation on Undulator Sources
- 1 to 13keV (15keV expected in the future)
- X-ray scattering and spectroscopy

system: solid surface and liquid surface

scattering:
- GIWAXS / GID / WAXS / XRD
- GISAXS / SAXS
- XRR from solid and recently from liquid surfaces
- GIXOS / diffuse scattering

XRF spectroscopy: XRF / Total reflection XRF / Standing Wave

Absorption spectroscopy: (refl-)EXAF, XANES, (GI-)DAFS

recently CDI

Sample environments
- full helium chamber for solid substrate GIWAXS
  - sample / beamstop / detector all connected by a bellow in helium
  - up to 200degC
- Langmuir trough system
  - helium flight path to the trough enclosure
  - one-el XRF detector from top
  - possible XRF from side
  - GID by Soller collimator + Pilatus 2D
  - reflectivity by last mirror to 0.5/A: supermirror 5% reflectivity up to 2deg incidence
- GISAXS on liquid and solid (1.3 to 4.5m)
- integrated UV-vis spectrometer
